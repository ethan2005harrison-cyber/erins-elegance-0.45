About Our Service

Erin’s Elegance is a calm corner of Cornwall; a place to relax while your nails are cared for with patience and attention.

Our Story

We started with a simple goal: beautiful results, kind service, and an atmosphere that feels cozy and welcoming. Appointments are never rushed; we keep the space relaxed so you can settle in, breathe, and leave feeling refreshed.

From classic manicures to longer pamper sessions, we favour soft shapes, natural finishes, and timeless colours that fit easily into everyday life.

Meet Erin

Erin is friendly, thoughtful, and detail-focused; she loves helping clients feel confident and at ease. Whether you are new to nail care or you visit regularly, you’ll be greeted with a smile and looked after from start to finish.

Warm conversation; careful technique; a gentle, personal touch

Our Approach

Healthy nails come first. We prep gently, choose reliable products, and share simple aftercare so your nails stay comfortable and look elegant for longer. If you prefer a minimal look or a bolder style, we’ll guide you to options that suit your taste and routine.

Hygiene & Care

Cleanliness matters. Tools are sanitised between clients; surfaces are cleaned throughout the day; the salon is kept tidy and calm so you can relax. If you’re feeling unwell, we’ll happily reschedule; your comfort and safety are always the priority.

Where to Find Us

We’re based in Callington, Cornwall. You’ll receive directions in your confirmation after booking.
