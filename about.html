About Our Service

Cozy, natural, and elegant — Erin’s Elegance is a little sanctuary for beautifully cared-for hands and feet.

Our Story

Erin’s Elegance began with a simple idea: salon-quality results in a space that feels warm, calming, and personal. We believe great nails should never feel rushed — every appointment is an unhurried moment for you to unwind, sip something warm, and leave feeling restored.

From classic manicures to luxury pampering, we balance meticulous technique with a soft aesthetic — rounded shapes, natural finishes, and timeless colours that complement every style.

Our Approach

We focus on healthy nails first. Gentle prep, quality products, and thoughtful aftercare are at the heart of what we do. Whether you love a natural look or something more expressive, we help you choose finishes that suit your lifestyle and keep your nails happy week after week.

Comfort-first • Clean, elegant finishes • Tailored to you

Hygiene & Care

Cleanliness is non-negotiable. Tools are sanitised between clients, surfaces are cleaned throughout the day, and we keep a tidy, calming environment so you can fully relax. If you’re feeling unwell, we’ll happily reschedule — your comfort and safety come first.

Products We Trust

We choose reliable, salon-grade formulas for strength, longevity, and a luxurious finish. Sensitive skin or specific preferences? Tell us when you book — we’ll make sure your treatment is a perfect match.

Where to Find Us

We’re based in Callington, Cornwall — easy to reach with convenient local parking. You’ll receive full details and directions in your booking confirmation.

Hours

Appointments are available most weekdays and selected weekends. If a time you want isn’t visible, add a note when booking and we’ll try to accommodate.
