Erin's Elegance Salon Policies

We’re committed to providing a calm, professional experience. Please review the policies below before your appointment.

Booking & Appointments

We recommend booking your appointments in advance to secure your preferred time. A valid contact number is required at the time of booking.

Cancellations & No-Shows

We kindly request at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling. No-shows may result in a cancellation fee.

Late Arrivals

Clients arriving more than 10 minutes late may need to reschedule or receive a modified service to accommodate the next appointment.

Payments

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash. All payments are due at the time of service.

Refund Policy

All service sales are final. If you are unsatisfied, please let us know before leaving the salon so we can address your concerns.

Health & Safety

For the safety of our clients and team, we reserve the right to refuse service to anyone exhibiting signs of illness.

Children & Guests

To ensure a relaxing atmosphere, we kindly ask that only clients receiving services remain in the salon area.

Client Data & Privacy

Your personal information is stored securely and used only for appointment bookings, reminders, and salon-related communication. We comply with data protection laws to ensure your information remains private and is never shared without your consent.
